STEP 1 – TEMPLATE

Abstract template Download
Use the enclosed pre-set Abstract form (A4). The settings are given in parentheses.

  • Font type is Times New Roman.
  • Margins are: top 3 cm, bottom 3 cm, left 3 cm, right 3 cm.
  • Type the title (bold, 14 pts).
  • Leave the blank line (normal, 12 pts).
  • Type the full name of authors (first name, middle name, family name)(normal, 12 pts), underline the name of the presenting author.
  • Use Arabic numbers in superscript in the case of different institutional affiliations of the authors.
  • Leave the blank line (normal, 12 pts).
  • Type the address or addresses with the appropriate reference numbers in front of them (italic, 12 pts).
  • Leave the blank line (normal, 12 pts).
  • Type the text of the abstract in single-spaced form (normal, 12 pts). All text must fit on one page.


STEP 2 – Abstract submission
